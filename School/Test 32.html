Chapter 6

‘You’re a clever one, I will give you that, but you forgot to listen. That’s rule number one, always be listening.’

I turned as a rag landed on the dirt beside me, bloodier than it had been in Fox’s possession.

‘This belong to you? You almost had me,’ the man continued, taking a step into the plot.

He paused, suddenly becoming serious. My heart pounded in it’s chest.

‘Now give me what’s mine and I will make sure you can still walk tomorrow’.
