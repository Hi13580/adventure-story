Chapter 5

A mumbled ‘fine’ was all I gave him as I retraced my steps back through the rows of rough housing to the man’s plot. There wasn’t much time, the beatings sometimes only lasted minutes and there wasn’t even a guarantee of that.

Doubt crept into my mind as I walked. It was stupid, there was too much left to chance. Earlier we had just been making the best of the inevitable but now we were being greedy and greed led to mistakes. And mistakes lead to…
