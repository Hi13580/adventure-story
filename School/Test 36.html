Chapter 7. A

Digging into my pocket for the bloody rag and the few wooden coins, I ran towards a boy giving him the coins before throwing the rest down the nearby alley, praying that the man will notice the movement as the boy scampared after his prize. Staying hidden I re-tied the bloody rag over the open wound across my arm before turning back to the rancid depths of the river.
