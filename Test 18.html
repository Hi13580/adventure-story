Part 3

Young, innocent and naive. Though our youths were simple and carefree, somewhere on this twisted path of life, those memories will shatter and become nothing but a shadow of longing. There was nothing in the orphanage but fear and oppression. Though it appeared like a place full of happy nothings, fear and terror clouded its walls within.

I stared at the wide eyed girl. She was shaking in fear and disbelief. As I turned to walk away, she called after me,

“Wait, please”
I glanced once more at the broken girl until it was only her broken form that shadowed the alleyway. My footsteps slightly staggered as I evaded the patrolling guards. Although I was aware that they could not see me, I was still unaware of the depths of my powers.
