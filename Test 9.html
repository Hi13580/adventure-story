Synopsis of the story

Set in the far future, the world's population has greatly depleted, so much so that society has resulted in life governed by officials.
These officials run society and ultimately decided that civilisation should live together, united in one city.
Within the city walls, surrounded by an unknown forest, society was peacefully governed by those officials until the discovery of people developing powers.
